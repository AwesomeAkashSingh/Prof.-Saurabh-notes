3. Hessian & Curvature

Classifying Min, Max, and Saddle Points

Explanation: The Hessian Matrix contains all 2nd derivatives. The Determinant of this matrix tells us the shape:

  • Positive (+): Bowl shape (Min or Max).
  • Negative (-): Saddle shape (Up in one way, down in another).

4. Taylor Approximation

Building Functions from Polynomials

Logic: We match the function's value, slope, curvature, etc., at a single point (0). The higher the degree, the further the approximation "hugs" the original curve.
